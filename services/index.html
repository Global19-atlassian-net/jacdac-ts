    Role Manager

    Assign roles to services on the Jacdac bus. Internally, the role manager stores a mapping from from `(device_id, service_idx)` to role name. Users refer to services by using role names (eg., they instantiate an accelerometer client with a given role name). Each client has a role, and roles are unique to clients (ie., one should not have both a gyro and accelerometer service with role `left_leg`). Role names can be hierarchical, using slash character as a separator. Examples: `left_leg/acc`, `left_leg/gyro`, `right_leg/acc`. If two roles share the prefix before first slash, it should be used as a hint that the services should be co-located on a single device (eg., here the `left_leg` "location" is expected to have both an accelerometer and a gyro service on a single device).
